About me

Let me introduce myself briefly. I especially liked painting since I was young. While painting, I naturally came across the design, and when I was in college, I created an electronic fairy tale book app that can only be seen on devices. After graduating from college, he changed his career to acting and singing, which he loved as much as painting, and joined the theater troupe before working as an actor. He quit the theater and is studying with interest in developing a front-end that is similar to his experience in producing an app book.
